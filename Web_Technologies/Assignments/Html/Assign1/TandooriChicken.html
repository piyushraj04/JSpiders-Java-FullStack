Description

A smoky, spiced, and juicy chicken preparation marinated in yogurt and spices, traditionally roasted in a tandoor.

Ingredients
  • Chicken (legs or thighs)
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Chili powder, turmeric, garam masala
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Oil
Method
  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices.
  2. Let it rest for at least a few hours.
  3. Roast in tandoor or oven until cooked and slightly charred.
  4. Serve hot with lemon and onions.
