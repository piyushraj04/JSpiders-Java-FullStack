Description

Onion Pakoras are crispy, golden-brown fritters made with thinly sliced onions coated in a spiced chickpea flour batter and deep-fried. These savory snacks are perfect with chai.

ğŸ´ Ingredients
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying
ğŸ§‘â€ğŸ³ Method
  1. Mix sliced onions, chickpea flour, red chili powder, turmeric, carom seeds, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually to form a thick batter coating the onions.
  3. Heat oil in a pan.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of the batter into hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with chutney or ketchup.
