Dal Makhani is a rich and creamy black lentil curry slow-cooked with butter, cream, tomatoes, and aromatic spices. A North Indian staple served with naan or rice.

  • Whole black lentils (urad dal)
  • Kidney beans (rajma)
  • Onions, tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Butter & cream
  • Spices (cumin, garam masala)
  • Salt

  2. Pressure cook lentils with salt and water.
