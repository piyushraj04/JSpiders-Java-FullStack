Description

Soft and spongy sweet dumplings soaked in sugar syrup, a classic Indian dessert for any occasion.

Ingredients
  • Khoya (Mawa)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom powder
  • Rose water (optional)
Method
  1. Mix khoya and flour, make a smooth dough.
  2. Shape into small balls and deep-fry till golden brown.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup with cardamom.
  4. Soak fried balls in warm sugar syrup.
  5. Serve warm or chilled.
