Aloo Gobi is a popular vegetarian dish made from potatoes (aloo) and cauliflower (gobi) cooked in spices, giving it a vibrant flavor and color.

  • Potatoes (cubed)
  • Cauliflower florets
  • Onions, tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (turmeric, cumin seeds, coriander powder)
  • Salt
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  1. SautÃ© onions and ginger-garlic paste until golden.
  2. Add potatoes and cauliflower, mix well.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices, cover and cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
