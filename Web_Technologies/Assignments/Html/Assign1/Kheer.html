Description

Kheer is a creamy Indian rice pudding made by simmering rice, milk, sugar, and flavored with cardamom and saffron.

Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Full cream milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder
  • Saffron (optional)
  • Nuts (almonds, cashews)
Method
  1. Boil milk in a thick bottom pan.
  2. Add washed rice and cook till soft.
  3. Add sugar, cardamom, and saffron.
  4. Simmer until kheer thickens.
  5. Garnish with nuts and serve warm or chilled.
