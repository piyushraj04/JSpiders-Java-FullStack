Shahi Paneer is a rich and creamy paneer curry made with nuts, cream, tomatoes, and aromatic spices. It is commonly served during special occasions and with naan or rice.

  • Paneer cubes
  • Onions, tomatoes
  • Cashew nuts
  • Fresh cream
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (garam masala, turmeric)
  • Salt
