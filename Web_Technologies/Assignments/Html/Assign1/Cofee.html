☕ Cappuccino Recipe

Description

Cappuccino is a classic Italian coffee drink, made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and a thick layer of milk foam. Perfect for a morning boost or a cozy coffee break.

Ingredients
  • 1/3 shot of espresso
  • 1/3 steamed milk
  • 1/3 thick milk foam
Method
  1. Brew a shot of espresso.
  2. Steam the milk and create a thick layer of foam.
  3. Pour the espresso into a cup.
  4. Add steamed milk, topping it with the milk foam.
