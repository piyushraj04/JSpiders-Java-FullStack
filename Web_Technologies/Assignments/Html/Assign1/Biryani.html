Description

A fragrant and flavorful vegetarian biryani, layered with basmati rice, mixed vegetables, aromatic spices, and fresh herbs. Perfect for festive occasions or a delicious meal at home.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, cauliflower)
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • 3 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
Method
  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then parboil.
  2. Heat oil/ghee, add whole spices, saut√© onions till golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and spices; cook until soft.
  4. Mix in vegetables and yogurt, cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Layer parboiled rice over vegetable mixture, sprinkle mint and coriander.
  6. Cover and cook on low flame (dum) for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with raita or salad.
