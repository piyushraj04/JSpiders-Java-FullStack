Description

Chole Masala is a spicy North Indian chickpea curry, made with chickpeas simmered in an onion-tomato-based gravy and a blend of bold spices. Perfect with bhature, rice, or naan.

Ingredients
  • Chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • Onions, tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (cumin, coriander powder, garam masala)
  • Salt
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
Method
  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with salt until tender.
  2. Saut√© onions and ginger-garlic paste until golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices; cook until soft and aromatic.
  4. Add cooked chickpeas and simmer to thicken the gravy.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with bhature or rice.
