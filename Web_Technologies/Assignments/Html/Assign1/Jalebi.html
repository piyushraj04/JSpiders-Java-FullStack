Description

Crispy, golden spirals soaked in sugar syrup, perfect as a sweet treat for festivals and celebrations.

Ingredients
  • All-purpose flour
  • Yogurt
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom powder
  • Ghee or oil for frying
Method
  1. Prepare a smooth batter with flour and yogurt; ferment for a few hours.
  2. Make sugar syrup with cardamom.
  3. Pipe batter in circular shapes into hot ghee/oil.
  4. Fry until golden and crisp, then soak in sugar syrup.
  5. Serve warm.
