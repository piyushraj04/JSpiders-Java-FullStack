Description

Kulfi is a traditional Indian ice cream made by slowly reducing milk and flavored with cardamom, saffron, and nuts.

Ingredients
  • Full cream milk
  • Condensed milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios)
Method
  1. Boil milk and reduce it to half its volume.
  2. Add sugar, condensed milk, and cardamom powder.
  3. Simmer till thickened.
  4. Pour mixture into molds, sprinkle nuts.
  5. Freeze for several hours, then serve chilled.
