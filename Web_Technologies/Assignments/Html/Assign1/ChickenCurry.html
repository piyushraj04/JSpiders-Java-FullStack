  • Onions, tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (turmeric, chili powder, garam masala)
  • Oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander
  • Salt
Method

  2. Add tomatoes and spices; cook to a thick paste.
  3. Stir in chicken pieces; cook until browned.

  5. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
