Description

A fragrant layered dish of marinated chicken with basmati rice, slowly cooked with spices under a sealed lid.

Ingredients
  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken pieces
  • Yogurt
  • Onions, tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices
  • Mint & coriander
  • Ghee
Method
  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt & spices.
  2. Layer cooked rice over chicken in a pot.
  3. Simmer on low (dum cooking) for an hour.
  4. Serve hot with raita.
