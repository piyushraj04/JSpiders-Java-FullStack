Description

Rasgulla is a soft and spongy dessert made from chenna (Indian cottage cheese) balls soaked in sugar syrup.

Ingredients
  • Milk
  • Lemon juice or vinegar (for chenna)
  • Sugar syrup (cardamom flavored)
  • Water
Method
  1. Boil milk and curdle using lemon juice to get chenna.
  2. Shape soft balls from chenna.
  3. Cook in sugar syrup till soft and spongy.
  4. Cool and serve chilled.
